Privacy Policy for Trivia Classic

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Heelmint ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Trivia Classic (the "Game").

We are based in Finland. By downloading or using the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The Game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All gameplay data (such as your progress, scores, and settings) is stored locally on your device and is not sent to us.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personal information.

2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The Game uses Unity Ads, an advertising service provided by Unity Technologies, to display advertisements. Unity Ads automatically collects and processes certain information from your device in order to deliver, measure, and improve advertising. This information may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • Device information (model, operating system version, language, country)
  • Information about your interactions with advertisements
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This data is collected directly by Unity Ads and is used for purposes such as serving relevant ads, measuring ad performance, fraud prevention, and analytics. We do not control how Unity processes this data.

For more information about Unity's data practices, please see Unity's Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, the legal basis for processing your data through Unity Ads is your consent (where required) or the legitimate interest of providing a free, ad-supported game.

When you first launch the Game, you may be presented with a consent prompt allowing you to accept or decline personalized advertising. You can change your choice at any time in your device settings.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the data collected about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or restrict processing
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights with respect to data collected by Unity Ads, please contact Unity directly using the contact details in their Privacy Policy. For any questions related to the Game itself, contact us using the details below.

5. Advertising ID and Opt-Out

You can reset or limit the use of your Advertising ID at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID, or Delete advertising ID

This will limit personalized advertising in the Game and other apps on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. The Game is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Game, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

Because the Game itself does not collect personal data, we do not retain any user data on our servers. Data collected by Unity Ads is retained according to Unity's own retention policies.

8. Data Transfers

Unity Ads may transfer and process data outside of the European Economic Area, including in the United States. Such transfers are made under appropriate safeguards as described in Unity's Privacy Policy.

9. Security

We rely on Unity's security measures for data they collect through their SDK. Unity uses industry-standard practices, including encryption in transit (TLS), to protect data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
